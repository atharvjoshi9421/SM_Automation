Who We Are

Established in 2013 at Dhayari, Pune, SM Automation is a manufacturer and exporter of PCB machinery, precision engineering systems, and customized industrial automation solutions.

Our machines are trusted for performance, reliability, and long-term value. From concept design and manufacturing to installation and service support, our team delivers a complete partnership experience for every client.
