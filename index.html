About SM Automation

Built in Pune. Trusted by production teams.

Established in 2013 at Dhayari, Pune, SM Automation manufactures and exports PCB drilling machines, roller tinning machines, PCB brushing machines, CNC drilling machines, and custom industrial machinery.

Every machine is developed around practical shop-floor needs: dependable output, serviceability, strong build quality, and timely delivery. Our team supports customers with machinery selection, setup guidance, maintenance, spares, and application-focused engineering support.
