Quality Control & Inspection

Our Quality Assurance Process

At SM Automation, quality is at the core of everything we do. Our comprehensive quality control process ensures that every machine meets the highest standards.

1. Raw Material Inspection

All raw materials are thoroughly inspected before production begins.

2. In-Process Quality Checks

Regular inspections during manufacturing to ensure precision.

3. Final Testing

Complete functional testing of every machine before delivery.

4. Documentation

Detailed quality records maintained for traceability.
